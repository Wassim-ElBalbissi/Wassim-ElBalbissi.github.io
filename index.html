Dosage Instructions

The recommended dosage is as follows: 1 ml per day, equal to about 10 drops, or as advised by your doctor. To measure the correct amount, we recommend using the dropper contained in the package. SiderAL® Gocce can be taken at any time of the day, either as it is or diluted in half a glass of water or other beverage served at room temperature. Shake the bottle well before use.
